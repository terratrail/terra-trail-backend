Features

Everything you need to run
your real estate business

From property listings to commission payouts — TerraTrail handles it all so your team can focus on closing deals.

Smart Property Inventory

Real-time tracking of plots, estate units, and all property types with live availability, pricing, and status updates.

Installment Management

Automate payment schedules, send reminders, and generate receipts — zero manual effort on every installment plan.

Agent Network & Referrals

Powerful referral tracking with transparent commission calculations and automated payout management for your sales force.

Multi-tenant Security

Enterprise-grade workspace isolation with role-based access controls. Each company's data is fully separated.

Smart Notifications

Automated WhatsApp and email alerts for due payments, new listings, agent activity, and every critical event.

Analytics & Reporting

Deep financial insights, sales performance dashboards, and custom reports to guide every business decision.
